FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

We are asked many questions about Pictune all the time. Here's a list of the most frequent ones. If you have any question or require any help, you can contact us.

What is Pictune?

﻿Pictune is a new language to communicate through img and music. Pictune turns your photos through a creative process into music clips, that you can share with your friends. You can choose the style of the music as well as the emotion you want to express.

You can think of Pictune as a tool who anyone – not just those with music skills – can use to create music. In Pictune, you make the same sort of decisions a composer makes when he or she is creating music, such as setting the composition style, instruments and mood, but you do so through a very intuitive process that doesn't require extensive musical knowledge.

Is Pictune free?
Pictune is completely free. You can get it at no cost at Apple's App Store.

How did the idea come up?
We love to take pictures and we love music. We believe that music is a universal language that we can use to communicate without words. Normally, in order to compose, one needs technical knowledge and years of study. Pictune is a simple way to get anybody closer to musical composition. Through something as easy as taking a picture with a mobile device and editing it, anybody can compose music.

How can I share my pictunes?
At this moment, we are developing new functions so that you can share your pictunes in our social network and in other communities as well, such as Twitter, Instagram and Facebook. Furthermore, you will be able to store each pictune you make in your mobile phone.
