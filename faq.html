FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)


What is Pictune?
Pictune is a quick, great and fun way of composing music through images. Take a picture and listen how it sounds. It is a new way of seeing and listening to the world. Currently, we are developing more functions into Pictune, so that you can compose the music that feels just right to you. Additionally, we wish you to share your creations with other people around the world. That is why we are also building a social network where it will be easy to publish your pictunes and see your friend’s pictunes.

How much does it cost?
Pictune is available for free at Apple’s App Store.

How did the idea come up?
We love to take pictures and we love music. We believe that music is a universal language that we can use to communicate without words. Normally, in order to compose, one needs technical knowledge and years of study. Pictune is a simple way to get anybody closer to musical composition. Through something as easy as taking a picture with a mobile device and editing it, anybody can compose music.

How can I share my pictunes?
At this moment, we are developing new functions so that you can share your pictunes in our social network and in other communities as well, such as Twitter, Instagram and Facebook. Furthermore, you will be able to store each pictune you make in your mobile phone.
