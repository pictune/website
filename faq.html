FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

This is a brief list of the more frequent questions we receive. In order to get
more information about Pictune, or if you need to reach us, please write to
contact@pictune.me

What is Pictune?

Pictune is a quick, great and fun way of composing music through images.
Take a picture and listen how it sounds. It is a new way of seeing and
listening to the world. Define how this instant sounds.
Currently, we are developing more functions into Pictune, so that you can
intervene the photos with filters and drawings. Additionally, we wish you to
share your creations with other people around the world. That is why we
are also building a social net where it will be easy to publish your Pictunes
and see your friend’s Pictunes. We are working at Pictunes to let you
experience some special moments through images and music.

How much does it cost?

US$ 0.00- It is available for free at the Apple’s App Store.

How did the idea come up?

We love to take pictures and we love music. We believe that music is
a universal language that we can use to communicate without words.
Normally, in order to compose, one needs technical knowledge, years of
study. Pictune is a simple way to get anybody closer to musical composition.
Through something as easy as taking a picture with a mobile and editing it,
intervening it with drawings, anybody can compose music.

At Pictune, we want to answer this question: How does this instant sound?


Are we hiring?

Absolutely yes! If you are a talented engineer or designer, we would like to
speak to you. The best way to get in touch with us is to send an e-mail to:
contact@pictune.me

How can I share my Pictunes?

At this moment, we are developing new functions so that you can share your
Pictunes in our social net and in other nets as well, such as Twitter, Instagram
and Facebook. Furthermore, each Pictune you make can be stored in your
mobile phone.
